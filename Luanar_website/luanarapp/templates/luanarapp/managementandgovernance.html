Management & Governance

As a vibrant hub of learning, research, and innovation, our university operates under a comprehensive framework of leadership, oversight, and strategic direction. The management structures of the University consist of :

  • Vice Chancellor
  • Deputy Vice Chancellor
  • Executive
    • University Registrar
    • Executive Directors
  • Academic Registrar
  • Faculties, departments, schools and such other academic structures as may be determined by the Council
  • Council
  • Senate
  • Students Representative Council
