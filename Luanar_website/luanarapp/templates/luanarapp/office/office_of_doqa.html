Functions of the Director of Qality Assuarance

  • Liase with National Council for Higher Education (NCHE) and the Malawi Bureau of Standards (MBS) on issues relating to quality and Relevance of programs.

  • Monitor and advise on the performance, implementation and the quality Management systems and criteria for promotion in the university.

  • Identify quality related training needs and delivering training and provide training tools and techniques to enable departments and sections achieve quality.

  • Establish networks and linkages with quality assurance regimes in the region and beyond to ensure standards are competitive.
