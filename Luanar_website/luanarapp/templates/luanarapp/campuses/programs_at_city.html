City Campus offers ten undergraduate programs (8 Bsc and 2 Diplomas) and two postgraduate programs from five departments including Extension, Agribusiness, Agriculture and Applied Economics, Agricultural Communication and Human Ecology. These departments belong to two faculties thus Faculty Development Studies and Faculty of Food and Human Sciences. Teaching staff at City Campus comes from these departments as well as other supporting departments. The details of teaching staff, their positions and qualifications are presented by the respective departments and faculties offering programs at City Campus.
