Vision

To be a world class University

Mission

To advance knowledge and produce relevant graduates with entrepreneurship skills for agricultural growth, food security, wealth creation and sustainable natural resources management, through teaching, training, research, outreach, consultancy and sound management.

Our Mandate

To provide quality education and training in agricultural and natural resources science, engineering and irrigation technologies, agro-processing, entrepreneurship, trade, climate change adaptation, sustainable utilization of natural resources and environmental preservation for socio-economic development.

Our Core Values

Innovativeness

Innovativeness

We are committed to conduct ourselves in a manner consistent with the highest standards of integrity. We aim to be honest, ethical and fair in all our activities. We aim to keep our word, deliver on our promises and acknowledge our mistakes. In all that we do, we aim to guard our good reputation as it is more important than any rewards.

Integrity

Integrity

We prioritize creativity and innovation in teaching, learning, and services, aiming to redefine excellence. Open to unconventional ideas, we embrace change and adapt to evolving societal needs, continually seeking innovative approaches to further LUANAR's mission through development, implementation, and evaluation of practices.

Environmental Stewardship

Environmental Stewardship

We aim to provide safe working and operating conditions for our employees. We understand our role as stewards of the environment and incorporate this value into our teaching, research and consultancy as well as all operations of the University.

Openness & Transparency

Openness & Transparency

At LUANAR we are transparent, accessible to anyone, anytime, anywhere; and responsive to new ideas and demands. We strive to lead by example, aligning disclosure and dissemination procedures with modern information management practices.

Professionalism

Professionalism

We demonstrate professionalism through collegiality, respect, and recognition for each other. We aim to exercise the highest professional standards in the use of time and resources. We recognize the necessity of many roles in the educational enterprise, and work in such a manner as to enhance cooperation and collegiality among students, faculty, administrators, and staff.
