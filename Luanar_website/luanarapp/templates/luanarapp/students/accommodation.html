Students accommodation

All successful candidates at LUANAR are required to find and pay for their own accommodation and meals.

For those who prefer to stay on campus, an application for accommodation must be submitted. If accepted, students will then need to pay the university for their accommodation.

Candidates who would like to stay on campus should apply for accommodation and, if accepted, they will be required to pay the University for the same.However, it is important to note that university accommodation is limited and allocated based on specific criteria, which can be obtained from the office of the Director of Students Affairs.

In instances where university accommodation is not available, there are private operators within walking distance of the campus. In such cases, students are required to deal directly with the owners to arrange accommodation.
