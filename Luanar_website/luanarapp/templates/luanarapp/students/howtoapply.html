LUANAR Admissions

LUANAR is a dynamic and vibrant community of learning that brings together administrators, academic staff and students from Malawi and abroad in an effort to provide sound and thorough teaching and research in order to address the present and future challenges of Africa.

Prospecting Students

Do you love to question and have an appetite for knowledge? Do you consistently achieve top grades in your class? Are you looking for an exceptional education in an environment which values individuals for who they are? LUANAR might be the place for you.

At LUANAR, Bunda College admissions are done by National Council for Higher Education (NCHE). Natural resouces College, Bunda City campus, and Bunda ODL admissions are internally done by the university.

Students from non-English backgrounds shall be required to write and pass the English Diagnostic Proficiency Test.

What is Required

For generic programmes offered at Bunda Campus, MSCE or O-Level credits should have been obtained in the current year or the preceding two years. However, limits will not be imposed on when MSCE or O-Level credits were accumulated for programmes offered at City-Campus, NRC Campus and through ODL.

English is the language of instruction at LUANAR. For that reason, all prospective students shall be expected to demonstrate proficiency in English in order to pursue their studies at the University.

For more information and further assistance please contact the international desk through the following email: Internationaldesk@luanar.ac.mw and you will be gladly assisted.

ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

Faculty Entry Type Requirements
All Faculties Diploma Malawi School Certificate of Education (MSCE), IGCSE ‘O’ level or its equivalent with credit passes in English, Mathematics, Biology and Physical Science (or General Science) OR at least 3 passes with grade “C” in Biology, Mathematics and Chemistry at A-Level or any comparable qualification.
All Faculties Bachelors (Generic) Malawi School Certificate of Education (MSCE) or IGCSE ‘O’ level or its equivalent with at least six (6) credits including English, Mathematics, Biology and Physical Science (or General Science) OR at least 3 passes with grade “C” in Biology, Mathematics and Chemistry at A-Level or any comparable qualification.
All Faculties Bachelors (Mature) Malawi School Certificate of Education (MSCE) or IGCSE ‘O’ level or its equivalent with at least four (4) credit passes including English, Mathematics, Biology and Physical Science (or General Science) OR at least 3 passes with grade “C” in Biology, Mathematics and Chemistry at A-Level or any comparable qualification.; And a relevant diploma obtained from an accredited institution of higher learning; And a minimum of 2 years of relevant post-diploma work experience.
